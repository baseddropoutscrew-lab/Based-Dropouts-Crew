About BDC

Community-First Movement

Based Dropouts Crew (BDC) is a community-first movement on Base. We started with the $BDC token, but our vision goes far beyond crypto hype.

Our Vision

BDC is more than a token — it's a movement to make education and opportunity accessible for everyone. Our ultimate goal is to form a DAO treasury, where the community collectively decides how to allocate funds to support:

Scholarships & Courses

For those who can't afford them

Young Builders & Learners

Who need a chance to chase their dreams

Community Initiatives

That embody freedom, generosity, and growth

BDC is about breaking free from outdated systems — and building something better together.

A culture of generosity, powered by community.

Roadmap Phases

1

Foundation

  • Launch $BDC token on kingofapes.fun
  • Seed liquidity and open trading
  • Build the first core community on Telegram & Twitter/X
2

Growth with Purpose

  • Spread dropout culture across crypto communities
  • Bring in volunteers (artists, meme makers, mods, educators)
  • Prepare the framework for a community treasury dedicated to education
3

First Impact

  • Launch NFT badges to recognize contributors (optional)
  • Introduce DAO governance — holders begin voting
  • Pilot the first scholarships or course support funded by the community
4

Full DAO & Global Movement

  • Fully community-run DAO treasury for scholarships and learning support
  • Expand programs to fund education worldwide
  • Build partnerships with learning platforms and grassroots initiatives
  • Establish BDC as the cultural movement for generosity and opportunity
